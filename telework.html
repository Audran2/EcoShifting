Why telework ?

Since the health crisis of 2019, teleworking has become a new way of working that has exploded in many sectors. Initially seen as a constraint, telework proved more useful and interesting. In addition to the lack of social interaction, a majority of employees say they are in favor of this mode, bringing them less stress, better comfort or even better productivity than in society. Many companies have adopted this new process, juggling face-to-face and distance. With this calculator, find out about your carbon footprint based on distance and your working days from home.

Who are we ?

Environmentally conscious, Eco Shifting is a small company interested in reducing everyone’s carbon footprint. Aware of the global challenges, we believe that everyone can contribute to the reduction of greenhouse gases without depriving themselves. With this in mind, we help you find sustainable and disability-free alternatives for your travel and telework, a new way of working since the pandemic. If you are also interested in ecology, join our action using our calculators.
